# AI-Driven Credit Delinquency Risk Analysis  
### Tata iQ – GenAI Powered Data Analytics Job Simulation (Forage)

This project was completed as part of the **Tata iQ GenAI Powered Data Analytics virtual job simulation on Forage**. The objective was to apply **data analytics, AI concepts, and business strategy** to address a financial services use case focused on predicting customer delinquency risk and improving collections strategy.

---

## Project Objectives

- Perform **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** to understand customer financial behavior  
- Identify **key risk indicators** associated with missed payments and delinquency  
- Propose a **predictive modeling framework** to estimate delinquency risk  
- Design a **scalable AI-powered collections strategy**  
- Ensure **responsible AI practices** including fairness, transparency, and explainability  

---

## Key Tasks Completed

### 1. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Analyzed the dataset using GenAI-assisted insights to explore patterns related to credit risk and customer payment behavior.

Key variables analyzed:

- Age  
- Income  
- Credit Utilization  
- Debt-to-Income Ratio  
- Missed Payments  
- Account Tenure  

EDA helped identify **high-risk customer segments** and provided insights for predictive modeling.

---

### 2. Predictive Model Framework

Developed a **conceptual predictive model pipeline** to forecast delinquency risk.

Approach included:

- Selecting important financial features
- Using **Logistic Regression** to estimate probability of delinquency
- Categorizing customers into **Low, Medium, and High Risk**

The model prioritizes **interpretability and reliability**, which are important in financial services.

---

### 3. Business Recommendations

Based on insights from the analysis, targeted intervention strategies were proposed.

Example recommendation:

- Identify customers with **high credit utilization and multiple missed payments**
- Launch **proactive SMS outreach campaigns**
- Provide reminders and payment assistance options

Goal: **Reduce 30-day delinquency rates and improve repayment behavior.**

---

### 4. AI-Powered Collections System Design

Designed a **high-level autonomous collections system** that combines AI and automation.

System workflow:

1. Customer data ingestion  
2. Risk prediction using predictive model  
3. Automated intervention actions (reminders, alerts)  
4. Feedback loop to improve predictions over time  

The system balances **AI automation with human oversight**.

---

## Responsible AI Considerations

To ensure ethical use of AI, the system includes:

- Bias detection and fairness monitoring  
- Explainable models for transparent decisions  
- Human review for sensitive actions  
- Secure handling of customer financial data
